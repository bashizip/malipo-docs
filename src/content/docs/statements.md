---
title: Monthly statements
description: Read and export the monthly history of your Malipo balance.
---

The Finance portal provides a monthly USD statement based on Malipo’s immutable financial register. Choose a month on `/finance/statements` to review activity or download a CSV.

## What the statement shows

- UTC period start and end;
- gross successful charges;
- Malipo fees stored with their transaction pricing snapshot;
- merchant net movements;
- settlements, refunds and payouts;
- operational closing balance.

The statement only contains your merchant activity. It never exposes Malipo internal accounts, provider costs, another merchant’s data, or internal anomaly reasons.

:::note
During the initial shadow rollout, the current month is shown as a preview. Published statements are immutable; a later correction appears as a referenced movement instead of rewriting an older line.
:::

All financial periods use UTC. Your profile timezone changes how dates are displayed elsewhere, but never changes statement boundaries.
